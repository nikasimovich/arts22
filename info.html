Overview

This is the website for Art of the Web (DSGN 234). This is an interaction design class taught by Nika Simovich Fisher. All assignments, notes, readings, and other materials will be housed on this website.

The class meets on Mondays and Wednesdays from 1:45pm to 4:45pm. This class will meet in Room ADDM 020 on Mondays and online on Wednesdays for the duration of the Spring 2022 semester. Per Penn’s COVID-19 policy, the first two weeks will meet online.
